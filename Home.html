TOP-OF-THE-LINE ACADEMIC PARTNERSHIP EMPOWERING LEARNERS WORLDWIDE

Limkokwing Global Alliance (LGA) is a power-packed learning platform delivering first-rate education and training systems to enable graduates to enter fast-paced job markets. The partnership involves over 280 universities in 80 countries bringing the best of education from East and West.

The collaboration cuts across the board empowering students, professionals and ambitious working adults in their career development. Recognized globally, the Limkokwing diploma, degree and post-graduate programmes enable students to acquire cutting-edge knowledge, industry skills and leadership traits to tackle the IR4.0 revolution.

With more leading universities across the globe joining to strengthen our collaboration, Limkokwing’s borderless class of students now have access to a wide spectrum of high-powered courses to boost their chosen path of study.

Costs are affordable and special scholarships are available to provide deserving students with the chance to attain their academic dreams. Indeed, Limkokwing Global Alliance is committed to transforming today’s youth into tomorrow’s leaders.
